About ProposalMate Wedding

Helping wedding professionals win more clients with beautiful proposals

Our Mission

ProposalMate helps wedding service providers — photographers, venues, planners, and caterers — create beautiful proposals to win clients effortlessly.

We believe that wedding professionals should spend their time doing what they love — creating magical experiences for couples — not struggling with proposal documents and client communications.

Our platform streamlines the proposal process with elegant, wedding-focused templates, easy customization, and powerful sharing tools that help you book more clients and grow your business.

Our Story

ProposalMate Wedding was born from a simple observation: wedding professionals were spending hours creating proposals in Word or PDF editors, often with inconsistent results that didn't reflect the quality of their services.

Founded by a team with backgrounds in both wedding planning and software development, we set out to create a solution specifically designed for the unique needs of the wedding industry.

After interviewing hundreds of wedding photographers, planners, venue managers, and caterers, we built a platform that addresses the real challenges these professionals face when trying to win new clients.

Today, ProposalMate Wedding is trusted by thousands of wedding professionals across the UK and beyond, helping them create stunning proposals that showcase their services and win more clients.
