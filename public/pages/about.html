About ProposalMate

Our Story

ProposalMate is a simple, powerful tool for professionals to create beautiful, customizable proposals. Whether you're a freelancer, consultant, or small business, ProposalMate will help you stand out.

We created ProposalMate because we understand the challenges professionals face when creating proposals. The process can be time-consuming, and without the right tools, the results might not reflect your true capabilities.

Our mission is to simplify the proposal creation process, allowing you to focus on what you do best - delivering exceptional work to your clients.

How It Works

ProposalMate offers a subscription service with a 7-day free trial to let you test out the platform. After signing up, you'll have immediate access to our proposal creation tools, templates, and tracking features.

Create professional proposals in minutes, customize them to match your brand, and send them directly to your clients. Track when clients view and accept your proposals, all from one convenient dashboard.
